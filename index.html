Welcome to the beautiful game of Chess!

Chess is one of the oldest and most popular board games. It is played by two opponents on a checkered board with specially designed pieces of contrasting colours, commonly white and black. The objective of the game is to capture the opponent's king.

Learn about life through the chess board!

Chess has always been a game of excellence and has produced some of the greatest minds of all time. They left us with some stories and inspirational chess quotes that reformed their life. What’s a better way to understand a person’s mind other than reading what they said? Here are the best quotes about chess by some of the greatest chess players of all time:

“Nobody ever won a chess game by resigning.” – Savielly Tartakower

“You may learn much more from a game you lose than from a game you win. You will have to lose hundreds of games before becoming a good player.” – Jose Raul Capablanca

“Avoid the crowd. Do your own thinking independently. Be the chess player, not the chess piece.” – Ralph Charell

“Every chess master was once a beginner.” – Irving Chernev

“If you wish to succeed, you must brave the risk of failure.” – Garry Kasparov
